Exercise: Completing Partial Circles of Evaluation from Arithmetic Expressions

Directions: For each arithmetic expression on the left, finish the Circle of Evaluation on the right by filling in the blanks.

  • (* (+ 3))

  • (+ (* 6))

  • (/ 15(10))

  • (+ (20(+ 3))(3))

  • (* (- (23)))

  • ((42)(/ 5))

  • (+ (45)(* ))
